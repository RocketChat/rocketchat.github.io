FAQ

  1. How long will Rocket.Chat Cloud be in beta?

    We are close to bringing Rocket.Chat Cloud out of beta, while in beta Rocket.Chat cloud is completely free.

  2. How long do Rocket.Chat Cloud free trials last?

    Every plan has a 30 day free trial. You can choose to cancel your plan anytime during the trial.

  3. When will I be billed after my trial finishes?

    You won't be billed automatically when your trial ends. Explicit permission to bill you and continue the plan will be requested.

  4. What is the billing period?

    All plans are billed monthly.

  5. When can I cancel a plan?

    You can cancel a plan at anytime and can continue to use your plan for the remainder of your billing month.

  6. Do you offer discounts for NGOs and educational institutes?

    Yes if you run or work for a non-profit or educational institute you may be eligible to receive discounted rates on Rocket.Chat Cloud Hosting.
