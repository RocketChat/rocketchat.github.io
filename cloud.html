Rocket.Chat Cloud FAQ

  1. How long do trials last?

    Every plan has a 30-day free trial.

  2. What is the billing period?

    All plans are billed monthly.

  3. When will my billing period begin?

    If you account has correct billing information and has not been closed, your first billing period will begin the day after your trial ends.

  4. When can I cancel my plan?

    You can cancel your plan at any time and continue to access your workspace for the remainder of your billing month.

  5. How do I report issues and get support?

    Email cloud.support@rocket.chat

  6. What reporting do you provide?

    You can view a workspace's total registed users, active users, channels and file storage used.

  7. What is the monthly uptime guarantee?

    We guarantee 99.9% monthly uptime and offer Medium and Large plan customers who contact us account credit to the value of: 100 x verified down time / plan monthly cost.

  8. Are there discounts for NGOs and educational institutes?

    Yes eligible non-profit or educational institute may receive discounted rates for Rocket.Chat Cloud.
