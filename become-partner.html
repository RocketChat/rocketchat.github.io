Partner program

Become a Partner

Authorized partners can take advantage of a direct contact with the Rocket.Chat team in order to boost their sales. From tech support to sales and marketing support, the Official Partner Program has five types of partnerships:

Installation Partner

Help Rocket.Chat clients to install and configure their servers to fit their company needs. This service includes brand theming of web, desktop and mobile apps; admin panel configuration; and cloud architecture set-up for scalability of on premise.

Hands-on Partner

This involves everything that requires coding Rocket.Chat. Its requires advanced knowledge on how to customize RC’s code. This means: the development of new features; integrations; bots; new applications for the marketplace; enterprise versions of Rocket.Chat’s code

Referral Partner

Partners with sales knowledge to sell Rocket.Chat’s Cloud or Support services, as well as enterprise features.

Self-Service Partner

Partners that offer their own hosting environment to deploy Rocket.Chat or personal support services.

Consultation Partner

Experts on Rocket.Chat’s code that are able to teach other companies to successfully design, build, and deploy the Rocket.Chat platform. This type of partner is certified by Rocket.Chat to provide education courses to end users.
