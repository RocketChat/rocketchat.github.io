Rocket.Chat Bots

The ideal platform for chatbot development, chatops, LiveChat and workflow automation

We make bot-making easy with BOTS Playground

Bot makers can now develop and create bots for their community or organization, on their preferred framework, and with their preferred tooling.

Rocket.Chat BOTs Playground empowers novice and pro chatbot developers alike to spend more time on coding bots, and less time on complex server setup and configuration.

Your first working chatbot on Rocket.Chat is only a few clicks away.

Using Bots

Rocket.Chat encourages chatbot and messaging automation integrations and has released comprehensive bot documentation to guide you through the bot-making process, from creating bot users to configuring the bot environment.

Although our Bot Survey is now closed, you can continue contributing ideas by joining the discussion on our #bots channel.
