Join the Rocket.Chat Community

Be part of a passionate, global and active open source community

https://open.rocket.chat

Rocket.Chat exists today because of the incredible support from the Meteor, JavaScript and wider open source communities.

Hundreds of people contribute to Rocket.Chat projects by coding JavaScript, TypeScript, Java, Swift, HTML, CSS and writing documentation.

Join our open community server to chat with the Rocket.Chat team and community.

{% include social.html %}

  • Star and fork

    Rocket.Chat is the leading open source team chat application. We have over 600 contributors across our projects. Star and fork Rocket.Chat on GitHub and help others discover the platform.

  • Use and share

    Download and install your own server and share Rocket.Chat with your friends and colleagues who haven't experienced the power of an open source team chat platform.

  • Squash bugs

    Join Rocket.Chat's passionate community to help us manage issues and squash bugs for Rocket.Chat Server, Electron, iOS and Android clients.

  • Document

    You don't have to be a developer to contribute to Rocket.Chat. You can create new or update old Rocket.Chat.Docs articles or tutorials.

  • Write a blog post

    Write a blog post on your Rocket.Chat story, tell us about it and we'll share it with the community.

  • Give a talk

    Give a talk at a local meetup or conference sharing how you use Rocket.Chat or how you customized it to suit your needs.
