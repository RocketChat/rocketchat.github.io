Causes we support

We offer special rates for people who do good things around the world

Non-Profits

Eligible nonprofits can receive discounted rates on Rocket.Chat Cloud Hosting.


Eligibility for special rates:

  1. Hold charitable status that can be proved with legal documentation.

  2. The organization must not discriminate by gender, race, ethnicity, religion, political affiliation, sexual orientation, nationality or any other kind.

  3. Organizations that are not included: private foundations, government offices, legislative or political organizations, organizations that aim to influence public opinion, churches or other type of religious association.

If you meet at least one of the above requirements and would like to apply for a concession please contact us.

Educational Institutions

Eligible educational institutions can receive discounted rates on Rocket.Chat Cloud Hosting.

Eligibility for special rates:

  1. Accredited educational institutions.
  2. Primary, secondary and tertiary institutions.
  3. Student organizations, organizations that are directly affiliated with an educational institution, school districts and regional service agencies.

If you meet at least one of the above requirements and would like to apply for a concession please contact us.
